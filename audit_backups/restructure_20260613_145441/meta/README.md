# Bird Scanner Build and Testing README #

This document outlines the development, inner workins, and testing process methodology of the bird scan of a region of interest, to gauge what species frequent the region. The overall goal is to develop a beneficial list and detection system for bird photography and field cataloging. 

## Rationale ##
The scanner system must reliably scan the internet and specific databases to get an underlying understanding of the bird species, that is the main goal. A secondary goal is to observe and log data bases with labelled or unlabelled data and images of the range of species, in hopes to feed a machine learning model to train it on these species, for eventual field deployment of the model.

It is assumed that there are people who have created these lists and labelled images, as the bird spotting hobby is filled with many technically aligned individuals with a pure obsession over birds.
